Anything But Standard - Stock founders Leonie & Jeroen

001 — Stock Office

“This is who we are”

How do you turn a very outdated canal house from 1886 into an ideal home and workplace?
This monumental building with majestic grandeur, located on the Nieuwe Gracht 41 in Haarlem, immediately captured our imagination. Five hundred square meters to get started with. So we can show our clients even better what possibilities there are.

Living and working in a magical environment. Here you can experience the world of Stock with all your senses.
It was an immense operation, but it turned out exactly as we had in mind.
Please come and visit us, so you can see and experience what is possible for the realization of your dream house.

Would you like to take a peek at the Stock family’s private home? Please visit our ‘Projects’ page.

002 — How we work

  • Creative Plan
  • Tailor Made
  • Supervision
  • Lighting Design
01

Creative Plan

We focus on purpose in design, meaning that we believe it is of utmost importance to tune into the personality and lifestyle of our clients. We study their daily life in and around the house and the way they make use of the different spaces. Combining this information with our Stock Signature, results in a one-of-a-kind, functional, fitting, and enchanting interior.

02

Tailor Made

Stock designs, from kitchen to walk-in-closet, are unique, perfectly fitting the house, and hand drawn in our own workshop. Each piece is then, in close cooperation with our partners, crafted out of durable materials, with love & passion for authentic bespoke craftsmanship.

03

Supervision

We oversee the entire turn-key project; from the first drafts through the technical designs to the last pillow on the bed. We have years of experience in supervision, and call ourselves lucky with a loyal team of dedicated building specialists. Our goal is to eliminate all worries and transform the process into enchanting memories.

04

Lighting Design

A state-of-the-art lighting plan makes all the difference. We strive for a perfect harmony between lichting, architecture, and interior. The beauty of precious objects and art is revealed in a refined way.
